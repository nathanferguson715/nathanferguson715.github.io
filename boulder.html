Bouldering

Bouldering is a form of free climbing done over pads placed on the ground. Bouldering is considered by some to be a more dangerous form of climbing due to the lack of protection from any ropes or harnesses. Bouldering climbs typical range in height from a few feet tall to over 15+ feet. Boulderers utalize spotters in order to help protect themselves if they fall before completing a climb.
